The Aleksander Foundation

The Aleksander Foundation was founded in 2005 by Acad. Aleksander G. Petrov, Ph.D., currently Director of the Institute of Solid State Physics, Sofia and Aleksander A. Petrov, Ph.D, CFA, an alumni of the FEB, Sofia University and currently Senior Practice Expert at McKinsey. The primary goal of the foundation is to promote and support the development of the Bulgarian university education through a number of initiatives including the organisation and sponsorship of conferences, seminars, workshops, short study exchanges and summer schools for highly qualified applicants.

quick bio


Mr Petrov is a Senior Practice Expert at McKinsey & Company in Milan. He is advising banks and institutional investors on their asset portfolios and strategies. Ralitza Petrova
quick bio


Ralitza Petrova is a member of the board of directors and the business development director of Interservice Uzunovi, a family business with 18 years experience in the retail of home appliances and furniture (over 320 employees and EUR 12mm turnover). Mrs Petrova is also the general manager of Intersolar Varna AD, active in the Bulgarian solar energy sector. Prior to that she was the executive project manager of Mall Varna - the first retail mall development in Varna. The mall was sold to an international investor and was the largest Bulgarian commercial real estate deal for 2007. Mrs Petrova was involved from the project inception to managing the acquisition; unification and re-zoning of the land; receiving construction permits; tendering for general contractor; overseeing of building process as well as all leasing negotiations with key tenants.
Ralitza has BA from the European Business School, London and an MSc in Political Economy from the London School of Economics. Ralitza speaks Bulgarian, English, Spanish, German and Russian.
