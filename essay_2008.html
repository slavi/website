The Aleksander Foundation Essay Competition
To win a: Fujitsu Siemens Esprimo Mobile LAPTOP
At the Aleksander Foundation we are carefully monitoring the financial crisis and its effects on the Bulgarian economy and stock market. We are a firm believer in finding opportunity in every crisis and have teamed with InvestorBG.com to present you one such opportunity to win an excellent laptop.



The Application

Only full time students enrolled in a Bachelors or Masters level program at the
Faculty of Economics and Business Administration, Sofia University are eligible to apply.

Candidates should send an essay in English (1000 words) on the following topic:

  "Opportunities and challenges for Investor BG.com through the crisis"

The winner is Ivan Todorov. Congratulations, Ivan!
