Risk Management Seminar
Sofia, 12th Jan 2008

The Event: The Aleksander Foundation, in cooperation with Alianz Bulgaria, announces a winter seminar on "Risk Management".

The Speaker: Mr. Georgi Momchilov, CEO of TB Alianz Bulgaria AD and a professor on Risk Management at New Bulgarian University.

The Details: The seminar will take place in the campus of New Bulgarian University – 21 Montevideo Str, room 131, building 1, on 12.01.08 (Saturday).
