Debt Markets
5 July – 25 July 2009, London

The Aleksander Foundation announces a competition for one full scholarship for Debt Markets, The London School of Economics (LSE) Summer School 2009, 5 July – 25 July 2009, London.


The Event

Eligible Applicants will be:
  In their 4th year/ Masters of Economics/Finance studies at a recognized Bulgarian University
  Have already successfully completed a course in mathematics and statistics
  Possess an excellent academic record and strong knowledge of the English language
  Able to demonstrate outstanding performance outside the academic field

The scholarship will cover:
  The cost of the course
  Three week accommodation at an LSE Residence Hall
  Return plane ticket to London
  Daily expense allowance
The Application

First Round - Documents:

All documents should be completed in English, clearly labeled (First Name, Second Name, Document Name – for e.g. Aleksander Petrov, Transcript) and sent to the e-mail address below.

  Curriculum Vitae (CV) of maximum 2 pages

  Translated and certified academic transcripts (no originals necessary)

  Essay (1000 words) on the topic of: "Opportunities and challenges for distressed debt investors. Discuss with specific examples"

LSE@aleksanderfoundation.org

Deadline for all applications – Sunday 26th April 2009, 16:00h


Second Round - Interviews.
  Five short-listed candidates will be notified by e-mail on Monday 4th May and invited for an interview.

  The interviews will be held on Friday 8th May at the Faculty of Economics and Business Administration, Sofia University

  Final decision on the winning candidate will be announced on Monday 11th May 2009
