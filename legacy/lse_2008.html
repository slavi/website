Advanced Corporate Finance Course
27 July – 16 August 2008, London

The Aleksander Foundation announces a competition for one full scholarship for Advanced Corporate Finance Course, The London School of Economics (LSE) Summer School 2008, 27 July – 16 August 2008, London.


The Event

Eligible Applicants will be:
  In their 4th year/ Masters of Economics/Finance studies at a recognized Bulgarian University
  Have already successfully completed a course in Corporate Finance
  Possess an excellent academic record and strong knowledge of English
  Be able to demonstrate outstanding performance outside the academic field

The scholarship will cover:
  The cost of the course
  Three week accommodation at an LSE Residence Hall
  Return plane ticket to London
  Daily expense allowance
The Application

First Round - Documents:

Necessary documents (all should be completed in English):

  Curriculum Vitae (CV) of maximum 2 pages

  Translated and certified academic transcripts

  Essay (1000 words) on the topic of: “Why are convertible bonds and preferred stock the financial instruments of choice to raise funds in the current market environment? Discuss with examples”

  Essay on the topic of: “Real Estate Market in Bulgaria – Myths and Reality” (800–1000 words)
